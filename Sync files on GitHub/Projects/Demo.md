# Final Demo

**Date of Assessment:** 16 June 2026
**Weighting:** 40%

## Demo Format
1. **Lab Bench Assessment**: The examiner will ask to see different functional aspects and assess our theoretical understanding of the implementation.
2. **Arena Demonstration**: A head-to-head competition on the simulated lunar surface to classify all the rocks in the quickest time.

## Pre-flight Checklist (Crucial)
- [ ] **Weight Check**: Total weight MUST be strictly under 750g (to avoid failing in the Weight Sensitive Zone).
- [ ] **Battery**: Fully charged.
- [ ] **WiFi Stability**: Web remote control connects instantly.
- [ ] **Sensors Calibrated**: 
  - Radio (89kHz) decoding works.
  - IR pulses (312/547 Hz) distinguishable.
  - Ultrasound (40kHz) detects presence.
  - Magnetometer correctly identifies Up/Down.
